About Us

Welcome to Brewed Awakening, your neighborhood coffee shop where passion for quality, community, and creativity blend perfectly with every cup. At Brewed Awakening, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s an experience. From the moment you step into our cozy, inviting space, we want you to feel right at home. Whether you're here to enjoy a quiet moment, catch up with friends, or get some work done, our cafe is a place to slow down and savor life’s simple pleasures. Our team is made up of passionate baristas who care about one thing above all: delivering the perfect cup of coffee. We source our beans from local roasters and ethical growers around the world, ensuring that every cup not only tastes great but also supports sustainable practices. We take pride in offering a range of handcrafted drinks, from classic espressos and creamy lattes to seasonal specialties and refreshing cold brews!!!
