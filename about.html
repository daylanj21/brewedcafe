Our Company

Brewed Awakening started by Daylan Jones is more than just a coffee shop, we're a community united by a shared passion for great coffee and friendships. Our coffee can be an energy boost to get your day started and can be bring comfort to cold chilly morning or stressful day. Coffee shops are popular social spaces where people gather to work, study, or meet friends. We provide a comfortable, relaxed ambiance with cozy seating, free Wi-Fi, and sometimes live music or art. They often serve as community hubs, offering a welcoming space for individuals to unwind, connect, or enjoy a quiet moment.
